About Garrard County EMS

Our Mission

Garrard County EMS is committed to delivering compassionate, efficient, and high-quality emergency medical care to the citizens and visitors of Garrard County. We prioritize rapid response, patient safety, professionalism, and community engagement, while continuously advancing through education and improvement. Our mission is more than a promise, it’s our dedication to making a difference, one call at a time.

Our History

Garrard County EMS has grown to become a vital part of our community’s healthcare system. Over the years, we have expanded our services, modernized our fleet, and continuously trained our staff to stay at the forefront of emergency medical care.
